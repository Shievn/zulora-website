Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI work?

Zulora uses a combination of advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) and our proprietary fine-tuned models. It analyzes your text prompt to understand your business type and requirements, then constructs valid, semantic HTML5 and Tailwind CSS code in real-time.

Can I use my own custom domain?

Currently, all sites are deployed to a free `your-name.zulora.in` subdomain. We are actively working on a custom domain mapping feature for Premium users, which will be available in an upcoming update.

Is the hosting really free?

Yes. We believe in empowering creators. We bear the cost of hosting your generated websites on our cloud infrastructure. There are no hidden hosting fees for sites deployed on our platform.

How do I pay for Premium?

We support instant and secure payments via all major Indian UPI apps, including PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, and BHIM, through a secure QR code displayed in the payment modal.

What happens if I run out of credits?

On the free plan, you get 30 credits. Premium gives you 1000 monthly. You can earn more free credits by referring friends, or you can upgrade to Premium to refill your balance instantly.

Can I export the code?

While direct code export is not currently a one-click feature, the websites generated are standard HTML/CSS. Advanced users can view the source code of their deployed site. We plan to add an official export feature soon.
