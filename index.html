The Mechanics

How Zulora Transforms Text to Tech

We eliminated the complexity of servers, codebases, and domain mapping.

01

Prompt Parsing

Our Natural Language Processor breaks down your request, identifying specific design themes, layout requirements, and extracting smart contact info (like your WhatsApp number).

02

Neural Generation

Instead of templates, the engine writes bespoke HTML5, Tailwind CSS, and Javascript. It injects high-fidelity placeholder images that perfectly match your niche.

03

God-Mode Customization

Your site is loaded into our proprietary Visual Editor. Click any text to rewrite it, click any image to upload from your gallery, and adjust colors instantly.

04

Subdomain Deployment

Select your unique yourname.zulora.in address. We instantly register it, host your site on a global CDN, and submit it to Google for indexing.
